About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at Ecole de Technologie Superieure (ETS), Montreal, working on resource management techniques using Reinforcement Learning approach, supervised by Prof. Marcos Dias De Assuncao , Prof. Julien Gascon-Samson , and Prof. Rajkumar Buyya . Before joining ETS, I was a researcher at Iran Telecommunication Research Center(ITRC), 2020-2022. During my Master’s, I was a research Development Intern at ITRC, supervised by Prof. Ehsan Arianyan .

I completed my Master’s thesis in January 2019, in the topic of Energy-aware resource management technique considering performance in cloud data center. Currently, I have published three research papers during my Master’s, collaborating with Prof. Rajkumar Buyya from Melbourne University. Along with my educational experience, I was the IEEE executive member at the Science and Research Branch of Azad University of Tehran (2017-2018) and Head of public relations in an environmental NGO named Clean Earth Lovers Association (2015-2017).
